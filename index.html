About Me

Hi, I am Wei-Chen Chen (zarcen)

I am originally from Taipei, Taiwan. I received my B.S. in computer science from National Taiwan University in 2011.

My research interests include human-computer interactions, ubiquitous computing, and machine learning. I have joined Smart Home Group of NTU Intelligent Robut Lab and worked with Prof. Li-Chen Fu since 2010, currently working as research assistant in Intel-NTU Center.
